तरंगीता

... where reflection begins
and ripples follow ...

तरंगीता is a piece of my heart, a quiet pause in the middle of movement. It is where I let my thoughts flow, slowly, honestly.

This blog holds reflections from my inner world, a digital documentation of my thoughts on spirituality, technology, and other personal moments that shape me. Some thoughts are deeply personal, some analytical, some still searching for words. But all of them, honest.

I write here not to arrive at answers, but to ask better questions. To listen inward. And to let my ideas ripple beyond me, gently, thoughtfully, and with an intention to touch.

तरंगीता is not about perfection or certainty. It is about presence, curiosity, and becoming.

I welcome you to be a part of my journey.
